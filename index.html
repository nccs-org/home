Providing warmth for those in need

We are the National Clothing Charity Society, a non-profit organization created to make the world a warmer, kinder, and better environment for all.

More

Those in Need

There are many people in our community who are in need of simple materials like sweaters, shirts, and pants. Most of these people who are in need are oftentimes struggling with financial problems and need all the support that they can get.

Next

The Community

By helping provide clothes to those in need, we are able to help many people stave off the cold. By engaging members of the community to help out for a common cause, we hope to not only support families in need, but also to create a more closely KNIT community.

How to Help

Choose any location near you and drop off your clothing donation in the red NCCS container. Containers will be emptied every week, but feel free to donate at any time.
